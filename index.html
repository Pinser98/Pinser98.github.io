oVRn

oVRn is a VR simulator that improves food safety by training users on the proper use of kitchen appliances. I won third place at CodeRED 2018 and worked with C#, Unity, and Oculus Rift in a 3-person team.

sGenerator

sGenerator is a training program that generates custom scramble states for Rubik's Cubes designed to improve training efficiency. It is endorsed by the world record holder.

TimSim

TimSim is a collection of computer simulations that run repeated random samplings to determine the probability of certain Rubik's Cube configurations occuring.

Decibel Display

Decibel Display is a device that will be placed in loud workshops. It measures and analyzes surrounding sound levels, and alerts users to wear ear protection at unsafe volumes.

Competitve Rubik's Cube Solving

When I'm not programming, you can find me solving Rubik's Cubes. I currently hold the world record for solving a Rubik's Cube blindfolded with feet. I've placed third at US Nationals, been ranked in the top 10 in the world, started a YouTube channel to teach advanced techniques, and wrote several computer applications to help me train. You can find my official results here. I also work with the World Cube Association to organize official competitions all over the world.
